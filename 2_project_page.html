Projects I have been involved in:

Formula 1 in school

I participated in the Formula 1 in school challenge where my role in the team was a Graphic Designer. In the team I was responsible for creating a portfolio, a team website, and posters which would help promote the team. I was also in charge of updating the team's social media pages regularly. As part of the team we competed in National level where we represented Scotland, and our school.

BP’s Deep water horizon incident

The Inspiration for this project was the BP’s Deep water horizon incident from 2010. One of the main reasons for disastrous oil spillage for 87 days was due to failure to close the Blowout preventer by automatic actuators. As an design enthusiast, I designed a mechanical system which could close the Blowout preventer manually and prevent such oil spills in future.
